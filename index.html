About Me 🌱

I am Islam Alsohby, a multilingual thinker fluent in Arabic, German, French, and Spanish, driven by a passion for philosophy, entrepreneurship, and STEM. My life’s work is a fusion of existential exploration and practical innovation, inspired by leaders like Viktor Frankl and the rich tapestry of global cultures.

My journey includes leadership in programs such as ELP and BIAB, where I mentor young minds, and active participation in sports like football, which fuels my resilience. As a STEM student, I delve into AI, data science, and engineering, seeking to solve real-world problems with clarity and curiosity.

Values such as integrity, lifelong learning, and cultural empathy guide me. Whether I’m pondering life’s meaning or coding the next breakthrough, I strive to leave a lasting impact.
